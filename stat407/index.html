Applied Multivariate Statistics

Lecture Notes by Di Cook


These notes were prepared for students at Iowa State University for an upper undergraduate level class in applied multivariate statistics. The main contribution of these notes is the material on interactive and dynamic graphics. Feel free to use them in your own teaching.
