Where do Awards go?

Move the cursor over points to get more information.
Can you find awards made to the University of Australia or Geoscience Australia? - Look for them in Austria! - Please note, that these oddities are not introduced by our rendering, but are recorded this way in the data. Similarly, awards with locations in China actually belong to Swiss companies, and awards with locations shown in Australia refer to companies and institutes located in American Samoa. These issues seem to be caused by an incorrect use of country codes during data processing.
