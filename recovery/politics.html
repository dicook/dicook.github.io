Recovery And Reinvestment Act

Which congressional districts are getting the money?

There have been some suspicions that funding has been directed to democratic congressional districts. Below is a map of the funding received by congressional districts across the country, coloured by party affiliation of the representative. Funds are pretty well dispersed between parties. And it should be noted that large amounts are going to state capitals, which are urban and perhaps more likely to be democratic districts.
