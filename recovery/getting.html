Recovery And Reinvestment Act

Who's getting the money?

Top recipients

The governor's office in California received the lion's share of $5 billion, and further awards to the departments of education and transportation in California were large.

The governor's office in Texas received $3 billion. Generally the funds are going to governor's offices, education and transportation departments.

Top surprises in recipients

Savannah River Nuclear Solutions, LLC received nearly $1.5 billion. This is an enterprise initiative of the Department of Energy.

CH2MHill Plateau Remediation Company received nearly $1 billion. This is a nuclear cleanup corporation! The bulk of the money may be targeted towards cleaning up the Hanford site, a decommissioned nuclear production complex on the Columbia River in the the state of Washington.
