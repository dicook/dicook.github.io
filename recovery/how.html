Recovery And Reinvestment Act

How is the money being distributed?

Funds such as those given by the Department of Education, Office of Special Education and Rehabilitative Services, to the New York Department of Education has been distributed to schools throughout the state. About 50% of the funds have been spent as of March 2010.

In general, we can see the nature of the distribution across the country: funds originate mostly from state capitals (blue dots) out to locations within the state, and some go across state lines.

Time line for funding

Funding really kicks in April 2009. Most states get a consistent feed of money throughout 2009.
